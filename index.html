About NSMOSA

37 Years of Glorious Service

N.St.Mathew's Public School has put up 37 years of glorious service in the field of education. The year 1973, saw the humble beginning of the school that was to make a name for itself.

A Journey of Excellence

The journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step. Similarly NSMOSA has risen from a humble dwelling to imposing buildings well equipped with latest state-of-art technology. The school is managed by the Society of Montfort Brothers of St. Gabriel.

President's Message

It gives me immense pride and pleasure to welcome you to the official website of NSM Old Students Association (NSM OSA).

NSM OSA is more than an alumni platform. It is a living bond that connects generations of students through shared values, memories, and a commitment to growth. Our association strives to strengthen these connections by fostering meaningful engagement, collaboration, and mutual support among alumni, students, and the institution.

Over the years, NSM OSA has grown into a vibrant community that actively contributes to academic excellence, professional development, social responsibility, and the overall progress of our alma mater. This journey has been possible because of the collective efforts, dedication, and goodwill of our members.

This website serves as a digital bridge. A place to reconnect, stay informed about upcoming events and initiatives, celebrate achievements, and participate in shaping the future of NSM OSA. I encourage all alumni to stay engaged, contribute their ideas, and be active partners in our shared mission.

Together, let us continue to uphold the legacy of NSM and inspire future generations with unity, integrity, and purpose.

Benefits of an Alumni

As a member of the NSM Old Students Association, you gain access to a wide range of benefits designed to support your professional growth, expand your network, and help you stay connected with your alma mater and fellow graduates.

The NSMOSA Old Students Association represents a vibrant community of graduates who have walked the hallowed halls of N.St.Mathew's Public School. Our association serves as a bridge connecting past, present, and future generations of NSMOSAites, fostering lifelong relationships and creating opportunities for mutual growth and development.

Membership in our association opens doors to exclusive networking events, professional development workshops, and mentorship programs. Whether you're a recent graduate starting your career journey or an established professional looking to give back, our community provides the perfect platform to connect, learn, and grow together.

Beyond professional benefits, our association organizes annual reunions, cultural events, and social gatherings that celebrate the rich heritage and shared memories of NSMOSA. These events provide opportunities to reconnect with old friends, make new connections, and relive the cherished moments of your school days.

We also offer various resources including access to our extensive alumni directory, career counseling services, job placement assistance, and opportunities to contribute to school development initiatives. Our scholarship programs and mentorship opportunities allow you to make a meaningful impact on current students while staying connected to your alma mater.

Join us in building a stronger NSMOSA community where every graduate can thrive, contribute, and continue the legacy of excellence that defines our institution. Together, we can create a network that supports each other's success and strengthens the bonds that make NSMOSA more than just a school—it's a family.

Wider Professional Network

Alumni associations are rife with opportunities for expanding a graduate's professional network. Connect with fellow alumni across various industries, sectors, and geographic locations to build meaningful professional relationships.

Targeted Career Services

Alumni associations often provide a wealth of career services to help former students find job opportunities and improve their chances of landing a job offer. Career fairs bring together employers from around the area, and sometimes further, so that graduates can meet company representatives face-to-face. Career counseling, seminars, webinars, and networking events may provide helpful information about the job market. Similarly, an association may offer resume and cover letter assistance to help graduates put their best foot forward.

Access to School Resources Post Graduation

In addition to career services, alumni associations may provide various resources to help former students post-graduation. This might include access to library materials, extensive journal and periodical databases, and even educational amenities on campus, such as certain labs and equipment.

Contact with Classmates

Being able to connect with former classmates can be another benefit to belonging to an alumni association. Whether this leads to a career opportunity or is simply a chance to catch up with old friends and acquaintances, graduates will benefit from alumni directories and online social media groups that keep the lines of communication open well after the caps and gowns have been removed.

Connect with Professors/Parent Departments

Connecting fellow alumni is one way to find career and social opportunities, but reconnecting with past professors can offer a wealth of opportunities as well. This is particularly true for research, as many professors at schools and universities are engaged in some type of academic research. Graduates could connect with professors in their field to gain worthwhile experience or guidance with a particular research topic.

Connect with the Community

Many associations partner with their local community so that alumni can provide assistance to the area while also having the opportunity to build relationships with community members and leaders.

Socializing

Alumni associations aren't always all business. They offer several chances to partake in social events, such as mixers, happy hours, and galas as well as homecoming events, football tailgates, alumni award ceremonies, and class reunions. Graduates can take a break from work and post-school life and reconnect with former classmates as well as other alumni, while making social connections that could lead to lasting friendships.

Giving Back

Graduates often want to stay connected to their alma mater and help future generations of alumni have the same opportunities and positive experiences they did. Alumni associations offer a number of ways to give back, including gifts, endowments, membership fees, and volunteer opportunities. Giving back to an alumni association is a great way to keep the school's legacy going and ensure that future generations of students have the opportunity to pursue quality higher education.

About Reunion

Celebrating Together

NSM encourages every graduating batch to organize regular reunions on campus and across cities. Reunions are a perfect occasion to relive memories, meet professors, and celebrate shared milestones. These gatherings strengthen the bond between alumni and their alma mater, creating lasting connections that span generations.

Annual Reunion Events

Each year, NSM Old Students Association hosts multiple reunion events bringing together graduates from different batches. These events feature campus tours, interactive sessions with faculty, networking opportunities, and cultural programs. Alumni share their professional journeys, mentor current students, and contribute to the school's growth.

Building Connections

Reunions serve as a platform for alumni to reconnect with old friends, make new connections, and stay engaged with the NSM community. Whether it's a small batch gathering or a grand alumni meet, these events create cherished memories and reinforce the spirit of camaraderie that defines the NSM family.

Get Involved

If you're interested in organizing a reunion for your batch or participating in upcoming reunion events, please contact the NSM Alumni Office. We provide full support for planning and organizing reunions, ensuring memorable experiences for all participants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NSM Old Students Association

How do I register as an NSM alumnus?

Click on the Sign In button at the top-right and follow the registration process. Once approved, you will receive login credentials via email.

How can I contribute to NSM?

You can contribute through scholarships, infrastructure support, student mentoring or by hosting events. You can also make a donation through our Donate page. Reach out via the Contact Us page for more details.

What information do I need to register?

You'll need your full name, email address, phone number, graduation year, and some basic personal information. Make sure to have your graduation certificate or student ID ready for verification.

What is the minimum donation amount?

The minimum donation amount is ₹5,000. You can donate any amount above this through our secure payment gateway or bank transfer.

How can I participate in alumni events?

Once you're registered and logged in, you can view upcoming events on the Events page. You can RSVP for events directly from the website. Make sure your contact information is up to date to receive event notifications.

How do I update my profile information?

After logging in, navigate to your profile page where you can update your contact information, professional details, and preferences. Changes may take 24-48 hours to be reflected.

How can I connect with other alumni?

You can browse the Alumni Directory after logging in. You can search by name, batch year, location, or industry. You can also join regional chapters to connect with alumni in your area.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, donations to NSM Old Students Association are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive a receipt for your donation which can be used for tax filing purposes.
